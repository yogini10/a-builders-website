About Company Page


40 years : A solid 'foundation'

"A house is built by human hands, but a home is built by human hearts", says the old adage. Founded in 1976, the ACME Group has invested the last 4 decades in fulfilling their promise of 'housing happiness' across Mumbai city. Over the years, the team and the projects have grown bigger, and the standards have been raised each time. Every ACME home owner has a happy story to tell. So when will you share yours?


7.5 million square feet: Homes in the making The ACME Group has come a long way. From building four-storey buildings to designing and executing mini-townships with over one thousand flats, the journey has been an arduous yet satisfying one.While you read this, 7.5 million square feet of housing is under various stages of construction across Mumbai city. Through the years, every project has been developed with the vision of world-class elegance and yet the intention is to build luxury homes that have a vibe of family and security in them. Is your dream home being constructed by us?


Our Values


Milestones of Mumbai: 100 landmark projects: In this 40 year journey, the ACME Group has scaled up its construction sites in size and volume. The Mumbai skyline has almost a hundred ACME landmarks dotted across its map. All new project buildings have been designed with care to ensure that the home-owner will experience "housing happiness" and the joy of staying there.


5,000+ ACME families: The ACME clan (or 'parivaar') is growing, with over 5,000 families residing in buildings and mini-townships constructed by the group, over a span of forty years. Their search for 'home' has ended in an ACME apartment. These families are the true brand ambassadors of the group, and stand witness to its credibility and commitment. Their keys to their homes are a symbol of their pride as home-owners. Are you ready to unlock the doors to your own happiness?
